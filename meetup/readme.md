
# Bucharest XDevOps Meetup — Resources

This folder centralizes materials for the **Bucharest XDevOps Meetup**: slide PDFs, demos, notes, and follow-ups.  
Its purpose is to keep everything discoverable for attendees and contributors.

## What’s inside
- `2025-11-06-first-meetup.pdf` — Slides from the kickoff session.
- `notes/` — Key takeaways and links mentioned during the talk (optional).
- `demos/` — Sample commands/code used in the live demo (optional).

## First meetup — purpose & focus
- **Theme:** From firefighting to foresight — applying AI to DevOps & SRE.
- **Goal:** Show practical ways to connect observability, automation, and provisioning with AI-driven agents.
- **Demo:** Provisioning-focused agent (“Hotfix VM”) and how it routes actions with context and memory.
- **Call to action:** Share your real-world challenges to shape upcoming sessions.

## How to use these materials
- Browse the PDF for the narrative and architecture overview.
- Re-run demos (if provided) and open issues for improvements or questions.
- Propose topics for the next meetup via pull request or issue.
